To be the global leader in IT Services. Building on our technologies, competencies and customer interests, and creating value for our stakeholders and customers. Goals

My long-time goal has been to focus on the product and the users. The ideal scenario is to be as close to the users as possible to observe and uncover their goals, pains & gains.
