My Mission In 2022

Mission

Learning about web development is kind of like drinking from a fire hose. Google “coding,” and you’ve turned the hose on full blast. This guide serves as a slow drip to get you acquainted with and intrigued by the world of web development. It’s by no means a comprehensive manual.

Vision

To be the global leader in IT Services. Building on our technologies, competencies and customer interests, and creating value for our stakeholders and customers. We’ll achieve this by focusing on the intersection of our client’s emerging needs and the acceleration of business and technological changes

Goals

My long-time goal has been to focus on the product and the users. The ideal scenario is to be as close to the users as possible to observe and uncover their goals, pains & gains. That is, ideally I want to be able stand up a product quickly and then start observing people using it so incremental improvements can be made.
